Our Story

From a backyard experiment to your favorite scoop shop.

🍦

It Started With a Dare

In 2019, our founders bet each other they could make better ice cream than the fancy shops downtown. Armed with a secondhand ice cream maker and way too much heavy cream, Scoops & Co. was born in a tiny apartment kitchen.

What started as a weekend hobby quickly turned into something bigger. Friends wanted pints. Friends of friends started placing orders. By 2021, we'd outgrown the apartment and moved into our first real kitchen.

Today, we still make every batch by hand using the same simple philosophy: real ingredients, honest flavors, no shortcuts.
